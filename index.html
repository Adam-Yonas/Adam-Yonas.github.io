About

Engineering hardware for real mission environments

I am driven by the challenge of turning spacecraft concepts into systems that can be built, tested, and trusted in real mission environments. What excites me most about aerospace engineering is the combination of physical design and analytical rigor: designing hardware that not only performs on paper, but survives manufacturing, integration, test, and flight.

My long-term goal is to contribute to missions that go beyond Earth orbit, especially through spacecraft propulsion, deployable systems, and advanced spaceflight hardware. I am most motivated by work where thoughtful engineering and practical execution come together to create systems capable of operating in extreme and unknown environments.
