About

A Summary of Me

I am driven by the challenge of turning spacecraft concepts into systems that can be built, tested, and trusted in real mission environments. What excites me most about aerospace engineering is the combination of physical design and analytical rigor, designing hardware that not only performs on paper, but survives manufacturing, integration, and flight. I enjoy working across that full spectrum, from modeling and structural analysis to hands-on fabrication, testing, and validation.

My long-term goal is to contribute to missions that go beyond Earth orbit, designing spacecraft and propulsion systems that enable exploration of other planets and, ultimately, missions that reach beyond our solar system. I am especially motivated by work that pushes the boundaries of what is currently possible, where thoughtful engineering and practical execution come together to create systems capable of operating in extreme and unknown environments.
