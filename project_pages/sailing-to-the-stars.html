Sailing to the Stars

Space Systems Design Studio

The Space Systems Design Studio (SSDS) at Cornell, founded by Professor Mason Peck, is a student-led spacecraft development group focused on rapid, hands-on mission design and real flight hardware. The team operates with an emphasis on building, testing, and launching small satellites and novel space systems, giving students direct experience with end-to-end spacecraft development. Through projects ranging from CubeSats to deployable systems and distributed ChipSat architectures, SSDS advances low-cost, scalable approaches to space exploration while maintaining a strong focus on flight validation, integration, and operating within real mission constraints such as ISS deployment and rideshare launches.
